# Cumulative Test System - User Guide

## What is the Cumulative Test?

The cumulative test is a comprehensive 20-question English proficiency assessment that evaluates your understanding of current and past lessons. It's designed to reinforce learning and identify areas that need more practice.

---

## How to Access the Test

### Prerequisites
Before you can take a cumulative test, you must:
1. **Complete today's lesson** - Finish reading and studying the daily content
2. **Pass the daily evaluation** - Score at least **76%** on today's evaluation

### Accessing the Test
Once you've met the prerequisites:
1. Navigate to the **Test** tab in the application
2. Click **"Generate New Test"** button
3. Wait 10-30 seconds while the AI generates your personalized test
4. The test will appear with 20 questions ready to answer

---

## Test Format

### Question Count
Every test contains exactly **20 questions**.

### Question Types

#### 1. Multiple Choice (MCQ) - 8 questions
- **Format:** One question with 4 options (A, B, C, D)
- **Task:** Select the ONE correct answer
- **Example:**
  ```
  Which sentence is correct?
  A) He go to school
  B) He goes to school ✓
  C) He going to school
  D) He is go to school
  ```

#### 2. Multi-Correct - 4 questions
- **Format:** One question with 4 options
- **Task:** Select ALL correct answers (exactly 2 are correct)
- **Example:**
  ```
  Select ALL correct sentences:
  A) I have a apple
  B) I have an apple ✓
  C) The sun is bright ✓
  D) A sun is bright
  ```
- **Important:** You must select ALL correct options and NO wrong options to get credit

#### 3. Fill-in-the-Blank - 5 questions
- **Format:** A sentence with a blank (marked with ___)
- **Task:** Type the correct word or phrase
- **Example:**
  ```
  She ___ to school every day.
  Answer: goes
  ```
- **Grading:** Minor spelling variations are accepted, but grammar must be correct

#### 4. Writing - 3 questions
- **Format:** A prompt asking for 2-3 sentences
- **Task:** Write a short response demonstrating your understanding
- **Example:**
  ```
  Write 2-3 sentences about what you did yesterday.
  
  Sample answer: "Yesterday, I went to the park with my friends. 
  We played soccer and had a picnic. It was a wonderful day."
  ```
- **Minimum:** 10 characters required
- **Grading:** Evaluated on grammar, completeness, and concept demonstration

### Topic Distribution

Your test covers three areas:

1. **Today's Topic (40%)** - 8 questions
   - Focuses on what you learned today
   - Tests immediate comprehension

2. **Recent Topics (40%)** - 8 questions
   - Covers the last 4 days of lessons
   - 2 questions per recent day
   - Tests retention and cumulative learning

3. **Weak Areas (20%)** - 4 questions
   - Targets topics where you've struggled historically
   - Helps reinforce challenging concepts

### Difficulty Distribution

- **Easy:** 7 questions (35%)
- **Medium:** 9 questions (45%)
- **Hard:** 4 questions (20%)

## Taking the Test

### Auto-Save Behavior

**Your answers are saved automatically!**

- Every time you answer a question, it's immediately saved to the server
- You can close the browser and return later - your progress is preserved
- No need to click a "Save" button
- You can change your answers anytime before submitting

### Navigation

- Answer questions in any order
- Skip difficult questions and return to them later
- Your progress is tracked (e.g., "15/20 answered")

### Time Limit

- **No time limit** - take as long as you need
- Tests remain active until you submit or advance to the next day
- You can take breaks and return to the test

---

## Submitting the Test

### Before Submission

Make sure you've answered all 20 questions:
- **MCQ & Fill-in-the-Blank:** Must have an answer
- **Multi-Correct:** Must select at least one option
- **Writing:** Must be at least 10 characters

### Submit Button

1. Click **"Submit Test"** when you've answered all questions
2. The system validates your submission
3. If any questions are incomplete, you'll see specific error messages
4. Once validated, the AI evaluates your test (takes 10-30 seconds)

### Validation Errors

If you see validation errors:
```
Incomplete submission:
- Question 15: Answer required
- Question 18: Writing answer must be at least 10 characters
```

Go back and complete the indicated questions, then submit again.

## Scoring and Results

### Pass Threshold

**You need 70% to pass** (14 out of 20 questions correct)

### Score Calculation

- Each question is worth 5 points (20 questions × 5 = 100 points)
- MCQ, Multi-Correct, Fill-in-the-Blank: All or nothing (0 or 5 points)
- Writing: Can receive partial credit based on criteria met

### Results Display

After submission, you'll see:

1. **Overall Score** - Your percentage (e.g., 85%)
2. **Pass/Fail Status** - Whether you met the 70% threshold
3. **Correct Count** - How many questions you got right (e.g., 17/20)
4. **Question-by-Question Feedback:**
   - ✓ Correct answers with explanation
   - ✗ Incorrect answers with:
     - Your answer
     - The correct answer
     - Detailed feedback explaining the mistake
5. **Overall Feedback** - Summary of your performance
6. **Weak Topics** - Areas to review
7. **Strong Topics** - Areas you've mastered

### Example Result

```
Overall Score: 85% ✓ PASSED

Correct: 17/20

Question 1: ✓ Correct
Your answer: B
Feedback: Correct! 'Goes' is the correct form for third person singular.

Question 2: ✗ Incorrect
Your answer: A
Correct answer: C
Feedback: Incorrect. The past tense of 'go' is 'went', not 'goed'.

...

Overall Feedback: Good performance overall. Focus on irregular past 
tense verbs and prepositions.

Weak Topics: Irregular Verbs, Prepositions
Strong Topics: Present Simple, Articles
```

## Retake and New Test Options

After completing a test, you have two options:

### Option 1: Retake (Same Questions)

**When to use:** You want to practice with the same questions

**How it works:**
1. Click **"Retake Test"** button
2. Your answers are cleared
3. You get the same 20 questions
4. Correct answers are hidden again
5. Take the test again to improve your score

**Benefits:**
- Practice specific questions you got wrong
- Reinforce learning on challenging topics
- See if you can improve your score

### Option 2: New Test (Different Questions)

**When to use:** You want fresh questions on the same topics

**How it works:**
1. Click **"Generate New Test"** button
2. AI generates 20 completely new questions
3. Same topic distribution and difficulty
4. Version number increments (v1 → v2)

**Benefits:**
- Test your knowledge with different questions
- Avoid memorizing specific answers
- Get broader coverage of topics

## Common Questions

### Q: Can I take the test multiple times?
**A:** Yes! You can retake the same test or generate new versions as many times as you want.

### Q: What happens if I close the browser during the test?
**A:** Your progress is automatically saved. When you return, you'll see all your previous answers.

### Q: Do I need to finish the test in one sitting?
**A:** No, there's no time limit. You can take breaks and return anytime.

### Q: What if the test generation fails?
**A:** You'll see an error message. Common causes:
- AI service is temporarily busy (wait a moment and try again)
- Timeout (try again)
- If it persists, contact support

### Q: Can I see the correct answers before submitting?
**A:** No, correct answers are hidden until after you submit. This prevents cheating and ensures accurate assessment.

### Q: What if I don't pass?
**A:** Don't worry! You can:
1. Review the feedback to understand your mistakes
2. Study the weak topics identified
3. Retake the same test to practice
4. Generate a new test when you're ready

### Q: How is the writing section graded?
**A:** Writing questions are evaluated by AI based on:
- Grammar correctness
- Sentence completeness
- Demonstration of required concepts
- Proper punctuation
- Minor errors are acceptable if the main idea is correct

### Q: Why do I need 76% on the daily evaluation to access tests?
**A:** This ensures you've learned today's material before testing on cumulative knowledge. It's designed to maximize your learning effectiveness.

## Troubleshooting

### "Complete daily evaluation with ≥76% to access test"
- You haven't passed today's evaluation yet
- Complete the daily lesson and evaluation first
- Score at least 76% on the evaluation

### "No active test"
- You don't have a test in progress
- Click "Generate New Test" to create one

### "Incomplete submission"
- Some questions are unanswered or invalid
- Check the error details for specific questions
- Complete those questions and submit again

### "AI service is temporarily busy"
- The AI service is experiencing high load
- Wait 30-60 seconds and try again
- This is usually temporary

### "Test generation timed out"
- The AI took too long to respond
- Try generating the test again
- Usually succeeds on second attempt
